1. Purpose

This policy defines how Clevertel SA Group securely generates, stores, transmits, and manages passwords and authentication credentials. The objective is to protect the confidentiality, integrity, and availability (CIA) of client and internal systems in alignment with POPIA and ISO/IEC 27001 best practices.

2. Scope

This policy applies to all clients, employees, contractors, and third parties who interact with Clevertel SA Group systems or receive credentials for services including (but not limited to):

  • Email hosting
  • Virtual PBX (vPBX)
  • Bulk SMS platforms
  • Web hosting and domains
  • Managed IT services

3. Secure Credential Delivery Standard

Clevertel SA Group does not transmit passwords via email, SMS, WhatsApp, telephone, or any other insecure communication channel.

All newly provisioned or reset credentials are delivered exclusively through the Clevertel SA Group Vaultwarden organisation:

üîê Vault: https://passvault.clevertel.co.za

4. Client Responsibilities

  • Clients must accept the Vaultwarden invitation and securely access credentials.
  • Clients are responsible for securing their own devices, browsers, networks, and user accounts.
  • Passwords must be changed immediately upon first login where technically possible.
  • Credential sharing outside approved secure systems is strictly prohibited.
