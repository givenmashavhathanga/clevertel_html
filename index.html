POPIA & Data Protection Commitment

Your privacy and data security are our top priorities. We are fully committed to compliance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA)

POPIA Compliance Statement: Clevertel SA Group is committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) Act 4 of 2013. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure the security, confidentiality, and integrity of all personal data we process.
Purpose Limitation
We collect only the personal information necessary for our services and business operations
Secure Storage
All data is encrypted and stored on secure servers with multi-layer access controls
Transparency
Clear communication about what data we collect, why, and how it is used
Data Minimization
We process the minimum amount of data required and retain it only as long as necessary
Your Rights
Right to access, correct, delete your information, and withdraw consent at any time
Accountability
Regular audits and compliance reviews to ensure ongoing POPIA adherence

Our Data Protection Practices

Lawful Processing: We only process personal information when we have a lawful basis to do so, including consent, contractual necessity, or legitimate business interests.

Data Security: We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption, secure access controls, regular security audits, and employee training programs.

Third-Party Processors: Any third-party service providers we work with are carefully vetted and must meet our strict data protection standards.

Data Breach Response: We have comprehensive incident response procedures in place and will notify you promptly in the unlikely event of a data breach affecting your information.

For more information about how we handle your personal data, please review our Privacy Policy, POPIA Summary or contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@clevertel.co.za
