1. Introduction

Clevertel SA Group (Pty) Ltd (“Clevertel”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) is committed to protecting the privacy and personal information of our clients, partners, employees, and data subjects in accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act, 4 of 2013 (“POPIA”).

2. Scope of This Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to all personal information processed by Clevertel SA Group through electronic communications, websites, platforms, messaging services, contracts, and service delivery.

3. Personal Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following categories of personal information:

  • Identification and registration details
  • Contact information (email, telephone number, address)
  • Billing and payment-related information
  • User credentials and access-related metadata
  • Messaging data required for SMS, WhatsApp, or API services
  • Technical data such as IP addresses, logs, and system usage records

4. Purpose of Processing

Personal information is processed lawfully and minimally for purposes including:

  • Client onboarding and identity verification
  • Provision of SMS, messaging, API, and managed services
  • Billing, payments, and fraud prevention
  • Compliance with legal and regulatory obligations
  • Security monitoring and system integrity

5. Lawful Basis for Processing

Clevertel processes personal information based on one or more lawful grounds, including: contractual necessity, legal obligation, consent, and legitimate business interests, as contemplated under POPIA.

6. Information Sharing and Disclosure

Personal information may be shared with trusted third parties only where necessary, including payment processors, regulatory bodies, and service providers, subject to appropriate safeguards.

Clevertel does not sell or unlawfully disclose personal information to third parties.

7. Cross-Border Data Transfers

Where personal information is transferred outside the Republic of South Africa, Clevertel ensures that such transfers comply with POPIA and that adequate data protection safeguards are in place.

8. Information Security Safeguards

Clevertel implements appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect personal information against loss, unauthorised access, misuse, or disclosure.

These measures include access controls, encryption where appropriate, secure credential handling, monitoring, and incident response procedures.

9. Data Retention

Personal information is retained only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by law, contractual obligations, or regulatory requirements.

10. Data Subject Rights

Data subjects have the right to:

  • Access their personal information
  • Request correction or deletion of information
  • Object to processing on lawful grounds
  • Withdraw consent where applicable
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator

11. Cookies and Website Tracking

Clevertel websites may use cookies or similar technologies to enhance user experience, monitor performance, and improve services. Users may manage cookie preferences through their browser settings.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

Clevertel reserves the right to amend this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on our website.
